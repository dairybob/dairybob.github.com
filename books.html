Milk Bottles of Saskatchewan

A guide for milk bottle collectors, this new book includes detailed tables of information about the glass milk bottles used in Saskatchewan by 65 dairies and creameries. A short history is provided for each dairy and creamery.

October, 2010
ISBN 978-0-9731348-7-2

Milk Bottles of Alberta Volume 2

A new collector's guide to Alberta milk bottles, this book contains descriptions of additional bottles not included in the original book. Also included are expanded dairy and creamery histories.

March, 2009
ISBN 978-0-9731348-8-9

Milk Delivery in the Battlefords

This concise book contains information about the three dozen large and small dairies that delivered milk in Battleford and North Battleford.

February, 2009
ISBN 978-0-9731348-6-5

Dairies that Delivered Milk in the Drumheller Area

The contents of this 147 page book provide a history of the 25 dairies that operated in the area from 1913-1959. The histories are supported by numerous local history book references, news clippings, corporate records, and pictures of artefacts.

October, 2007
ISBN 978-0-9731348-5-8

The Dairies of Red Deer

A very detailed history of the large and small dairies that operated in Red Deer, Alberta is set out in this 80-page book. Information ont he dairy operations and their location, along with pictures of some buildings and artefacts are included.

February, 2007
ISBN 0-9731348-4-4

Milk Bottles of Alberta

A 112 page book providing detailed listings, photos, and sketches of the bottles used by the 80 dairies in Alberta that used bottles with their names on them. Also includes short histories of the dairies and creameries that used the bottles. This book is an excellent colelctor's reference guide.

April, 2006
ISBN 0-9731348-3-6

Dairies of Edmonton (1905-1955)

This 106 page book provides a listing of the 134 dairies that operated in Edmonton from 1905 to 1955, with photos and individual histories, including descriptions and photos of available artefacts. Please note that there are two supplements, published in August 2006 and April 2008, that go with this book.

February, 2005
ISBN 0-9731348-2-8

The Milk Bottles of Edmonton City Dairy

This 80 page book provides information about Edmonton City Dairy (ECD), details and photos of the bottles used by the Dairy, and details and photos of a myriad of promotional items, boxes and cartons and other business items used by ECD.

Second edition - April, 2004
ISBN 0-9731348-1-X
